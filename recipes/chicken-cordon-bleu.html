Chicken Cordon Bleu

Barber Foods Chicken Cordon Bleu before by Willis Lam, used under CC BY-SA 2.0

Description

Chicken Cordon Bleu is a classic dish that features breaded chicken breasts stuffed with ham and Swiss cheese, then baked to golden perfection. This recipe is perfect for a special dinner or when you want to impress your guests with a delicious and elegant meal.

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 35 minutes | Total Time: 45 minutes

Source: allrecipes

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  4. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper.
  6. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast.
  7. Roll up each breast and secure with toothpicks.
  8. Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese slice on top of each breast.
  11. Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
